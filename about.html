About Us

About MMT (Mai Martaba Tex)

Mai Martaba Tex (MMT) is a Nigerian textile company dedicated to providing high-quality fabrics and fashion materials. We specialize in premium textiles including wax prints, lace, wool, and modern fashion fabrics. At MMT, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design to meet the needs of designers, retailers, and individuals across Nigeria and beyond.

Biography of the CEO

Mahmud Ibrahim is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Mai Martaba Tex (MMT). He is a visionary entrepreneur with a strong passion for the textile and fashion industry. Through dedication, innovation, and hard work, he has built MMT into a trusted name known for quality and reliability.

Under his leadership, MMT continues to grow by embracing creativity, customer satisfaction, and sustainable business practices. His goal is to expand Nigerian textiles to the global market while empowering local artisans and businesses.
